Privacy Policy for AllTrails Real Time Permit Checker

Last updated: June 17, 2025

This privacy policy describes how AllTrails Real Time Permit Checker ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and shares user data.

Data Collection and Use

  • The extension collects and stores user preferences such as permit dates, group sizes, and whether you want to only see trails with available permits.
  • The extension reads information from AllTrails pages you visit to identify trails and display permit information.
  • The extension makes API calls to permit reservation systems to check real-time availability.

Data Storage

  • All user preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing

  • The extension does not share your personal data with any third parties.
  • API calls to permit systems only request availability information and do not send personal user data.

Data Retention

  • User preferences are stored until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data.

User Rights

  • You can view and modify your stored preferences through the extension interface.
  • You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact serengupt@gmail.com.
